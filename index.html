01 // About

Who I Am

I'm a Computer Vision and ML Engineer with 4.5 years of experience building perception and intelligence systems for real-world robots and autonomous machines.

My work spans commercial, industrial, and military domains — from training panoptic segmentation models for warehouse digital twins at KION Group, to leading an 8-person team building a counter-drone system for the Indian military at Astr Defence.

I hold a Master's in Autonomous Systems from Bonn-Rhein-Sieg University of Applied Sciences and have published at IROS and RoboCup Symposium through research at the University of Bonn.

Outside algorithms, I care about robots that work in the real world — messy, ambiguous, uncontrolled environments where clever engineering beats brute compute.
